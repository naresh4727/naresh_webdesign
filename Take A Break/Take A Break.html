Welcome
to TAKE A BREAK

We are certified under the government protocol of Himachal Pradesh tourism act 2002.

Take a break was established in july 2017. We operate to provide our traveling mates a hustle free traveling environment. Our motto is not related to business sustainability only but to create a travelers community worldwide so that we can travel with ease. We may be new to this field but our organizers are well experienced and have tie ups with various ventures dealing in tourism sectors across the country.

We moreover provide services according to our clients budget and can customize our packages with respect to timings, interests and affordability of our customers.

Treking

The most satisfactory adventure for travelers wanting to explore the unexplored. Walking to places where very few manage to escape. We organize such treks in Himalayas differentiated into various levels depending upon timing, difficulty and challenge.

Camping

Bonfire, riverside, music and star gazing what else could it be. Outdoors have always been thrilling for adventure lovers. Spend quality time at various peaceful locations with your mates cooking and cheering in the open-air. We have link ups with various camping organizers around the valley.

Guide

If you don't want to buy trek or trip from us but still wants a local person to accompany you. You can hire a guide on daily wages of 800/- . The person will be friendly, experienced and educated with good communication skills

Taxi

Get in touch with us to book the best vehicles for your trip. We provide pickup and drops all-round India. From air tickets to Volvo we have a mass network of mediums connecting Himachal Pradesh with the entire country.

Hybrid

These type of tours involve different type of thrills in one go. These modified travel packages consists of local sightseeing, small treks, camping and adventure sports activities. Traveler can book modified itinerary according to there preference and interests.

Stays

Ongoing trend for homestays across himachal is at peak at the moment. It gives a magical chance to experience the local culture and traditions. No better way to get a glimpse of the particular area by staying like a native and experiencing the lifestyle opted by the people of that particular place. Book various such homestay facilities across the valley.

Photo

Join our representatives to explore various off tourist destinations accompanied by your camera. Himalayas have been the most preferred destination for photography lovers. We organize photo walks across the valley with transportation and food facilities.

Adventure

Challenge your fitness and spirit by trying various adventure sports activities available around the valley. We organise rafting, paragliding, rock climbing, rappelling, skiing, river crossing etc

Hire

We provide camping gears on rental basis on daily charge. Good quality tents, sleeping bags and matrices on nominal expenses. BIKE and cycle hire also available.

Food

Enjoy Pahari dishes made with traditional style and farm fresh food.
